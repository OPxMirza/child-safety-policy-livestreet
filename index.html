Child Safety Standards Policy

App Name: LiveStreet

Developer: R App Studios

LiveStreet is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have a strict zero-tolerance policy toward any content or behavior that endangers minors.

1. Zero Tolerance for CSAE

We strictly prohibit:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
  • Any sexual or exploitative content involving minors
  • Grooming, predatory behavior, or inappropriate interactions with minors

2. User Safety & Reporting

To maintain a safe environment:

  • Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app
  • Reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team
  • We take immediate action against violating accounts

3. Enforcement Actions

If violations are detected:

  • Content will be removed immediately
  • Accounts will be suspended or permanently banned
  • We may restrict access without prior notice

4. Cooperation with Authorities

We comply with all applicable laws regarding child protection. Any confirmed cases of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) will be reported to relevant law enforcement authorities and organizations.
