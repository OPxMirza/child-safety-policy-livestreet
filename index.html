Child Safety Standards Policy – MotoGP Game

Our app is committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have zero tolerance for any content or behavior that exploits or harms children.

We do not allow users to upload, share, or distribute content involving minors in a harmful or exploitative manner. Any violations result in immediate action.

Users can report inappropriate content within the app. We review reports promptly.

We comply with all applicable child safety laws and report CSAM to relevant authorities.
